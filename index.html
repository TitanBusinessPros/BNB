Blessings Not Burdens was founded by brothers Jeremiah Wooten and Joshua Wooten with a simple but powerful mission: to transform hardship into hope by restoring dignity, safety, and stability to families who are doing their best—but need a hand up, not a handout. Rooted in faith, integrity, and service, Blessings Not Burdens exists to renovate homes, rebuild lives, and strengthen communities through strategic construction, charitable partnerships, and compassionate outreach. The organization focuses on families facing financial strain, unsafe living conditions, or unexpected hardship—ensuring that no burden has to be carried alone.


Jeremiah Wooten brings extensive leadership in construction, business operations, and nonprofit governance. As a contractor and community leader, Jeremiah has spent years building both structures and systems—overseeing projects that demand excellence, accountability, and stewardship. His passion lies in creating sustainable solutions that move families from survival to stability.


Joshua Wooten brings a heart for people, service, and long-term impact. With a strong background in organization, project coordination, ministry, and community engagement, Joshua ensures that each family is treated with dignity, transparency, and care. His focus is on building trust, partnerships, and pathways that create lasting change beyond the renovation itself.


Together, the Wooten brothers lead Blessings Not Burdens with the belief that generosity is not measured only in dollars, but in action, compassion, and commitment. Their vision is to see communities where homes are safe, families are supported, and burdens are consistently converted into blessings. Since day one, they’ve united local churches, contractors, and volunteers to respond to urgent housing needs. Every renovation is more than nails and drywall — it’s a declaration that families matter, that hope lives here, and that Oklahoma families will always have a place to turn.

What We Do: Strategic Home & Community Renewal

From emergency repairs to complete home transformations — we serve families in need with excellence and compassion.

Critical Home Repairs

Roof leaks, electrical hazards, plumbing failures, and structural issues — we respond quickly to make homes safe again. No cost to qualifying families.

Accessibility Upgrades

Ramps, bathroom modifications, and mobility improvements for elderly or disabled family members, restoring independence and safety.

Charitable Partnerships

We collaborate with local businesses, churches, and civic groups to amplify impact — turning donated materials and volunteer labor into life-changing renovations.

Full Home Renovation

For families in severe disrepair or after crisis events, we coordinate whole-home makeovers, ensuring warmth, security, and a fresh start.

Emergency Weatherization

Heating/cooling repair, window sealing, insulation — protecting families from extreme Oklahoma weather, reducing energy bills.

Mold & Moisture Remediation

Health-focused interventions to remove mold, fix leaks, and create a breathable, safe indoor environment for children and elders.

How Blessings Not Burdens Changes Lives — Real Impact

Since our founding, we’ve completed over 85 critical home repairs, including 12 full home renovations for families in crisis. In 2024 alone, we provided emergency heating repairs to 22 households, built 8 wheelchair ramps, and partnered with local suppliers to deliver more than $175,000 in donated construction services. The vision of Jeremiah and Joshua Wooten has grown into a movement where every nail driven represents a burden turned into blessing. We are a 501(c)(3) in formation, fueled by grassroots support and faith. Our long-term plan: expand housing stability programs, add transitional repair services, and equip families with home maintenance skills. Located in the heart of Oklahoma, we bridge the gap between those who want to give and families who need a safe place to call home.
